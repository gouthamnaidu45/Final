Right to Vote

The right to vote is a fundamental right in a democratic society. It allows citizens to participate in the decision-making process and choose their leaders.

Importance of Voting

  • Ensures accountability of elected representatives
  • Promotes democratic participation and engagement
  • Protects individual rights and freedoms
  • Shapes the future of the country

Eligibility to Vote

To be eligible to vote, you typically need to:

  • Be a citizen of the country
  • Meet the age requirement (usually 18 years old)
  • Be registered to vote
  • Meet other requirements as specified by the election laws
